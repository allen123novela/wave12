        <section id="vmi">
            <h2>Verify Medical Info (VMI)</h2>
            <p>Call script and questions:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Was patient seen for (diagnosis) or after (LDW)?</li>
                <li>First and last visit after LDW?</li>
                <li>Hospitalization or SX?</li>
                <li>TX Plan?</li>
                <li>R&Ls?</li>
                <li>ERTW?</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section id="bh">
            <h2>Behavioral Health (BH)</h2>
            <p>Behavioral health verification questions:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Seen after LDW for DX?</li>
                <li>FOV, LOV, NOV dates?</li>
                <li>Current symptoms?</li>
                <li>Referred to PHP/IOP?</li>
                <li>Psychiatric R&Ls?</li>
                <li>ERTW?</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section id="surgery">
            <h2>Surgery Verification</h2>
            <ul>
                <li>Did patient have SX for (Diagnosis) after (LDW)?</li>
                <li>SX type: Open or Lap?</li>
                <li>Inpatient or Outpatient?</li>
                <li>ERTW?</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section id="delivery">
            <h2>Delivery Verification</h2>
            <ul>
                <li>DEL Date: Vaginal or C-Section?</li>
                <li>Admission and Discharge Dates?</li>
                <li>R&Ls after delivery?</li>
                <li>Complications or normal?</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section id="lvm">
            <h2>LVM Spiels</h2>
            <h3>Standard:</h3>
            <p>"This is ALLEN with Unum Disability... reference (claim number)."</p>

            <h3>With Dedicated Line:</h3>
            <p>"We are faxing over paperwork. If forms are returned, no call is needed..."</p>

            <h3>EE Call:</h3>
            <p>"I’m working on your short-term disability claim. I need your AP’s info..."</p>
        </section>
